📷

🍳

Smart Recipes

Get personalized recipes that match your exact ingredients AND hit your nutrition targets within 15% tolerance. Breakfast, lunch, dinner, or snack.

How It Works

From fridge to plate
in four steps.

The complete kitchen loop — no other app connects all four steps.

01
📸

Scan

Take photos of your fridge, scan receipts, or use the barcode scanner. AI detects everything.

02
🍽️

Cook

Get AI recipes matching your ingredients and goals. Step-by-step cooking mode keeps your screen on.

03
🔄

Update

Pantry auto-updates after cooking. Used ingredients are deducted. Leftovers get new recipe ideas.

04
🛒

Restock

Missing items go to your shopping list. Smart suggestions remind you what to buy. Check off and add to pantry.
